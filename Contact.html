Browse by age

Screen time concerns look different at every stage

Choose an age group to browse the Directory filtered for that stage.

Ages 0 to 5

Early childhood is when screen habits form. Research on brain development and language points to the importance of limiting screens and increasing active play at this stage.

Ages 6 to 12

Gaming, YouTube, and after school device time are common challenges. This section focuses on structured off-screen activities, tutoring, and family routine support.

Teens

Social media, late night phone use, and sleep disruption are well documented in this age group. This section connects families with resources focused on habits, real world activities, and family communication.
